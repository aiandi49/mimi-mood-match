Live Platform · Internal Review · Revived Concept
AI&I today shared Mimi's Mood Match, a live full-stack mood-matching app built entirely around Mariah Carey's catalog: a mood check-in, a scoring engine, a personal library, and streaks. "The songs already do the emotional work — the app's whole job is getting a listener to the right one faster," said developer Lamar Myers. The concept is documented here as a technical case study, with any real launch contingent on licensing from the actual rights holders.
